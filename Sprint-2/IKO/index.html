About Us

"Who Are We And What Do We Do?"

Welcome! We are the MR-FIRST story founded by a group of passionate foodies who love delicious and carefully prepared rice dishes. Our aim when we set out was to present pilaf, one of everyone's favorite flavors, to our customers by preparing it with our special recipes and quality ingredients. We offer you a unique experience by blending pilaf, the indispensable flavor of traditional Turkish cuisine, with our modern and innovative approaches.

As MR-FIRST, we take special care to prepare each type of rice with the freshest and highest quality ingredients. By combining our traditional recipes with modern technology and taste, we create delicious flavors that suit your taste. With high standards of hygiene and quality control, we guarantee the freshness and taste of each of our rice dishes.

Customer satisfaction is always a priority for us. We work to meet and even exceed the expectations of our valued customers. We are constantly improving ourselves to offer the best service and the most delicious pilaf in every order.

We invite you to join the MR-FIRST family and discover the most special rice flavors. Whether alone or with your loved ones, a warm, delicious and satisfying experience always awaits you with MR-FIRST

Thank you for choosing us! MR-FIRST
